## 📘 About bilgisayar-mimarisi

bilgisayar-mimarisi is an open-source Turkish textbook about computer architecture. It focuses on the RISC-V instruction set, a popular approach used in education and research. This book offers a clear and practical introduction to computer architecture concepts, designed for students, educators, and anyone interested in learning at their own pace.

The content follows a structured approach, starting from the basics of digital logic and moving to processor design and assembly language programming. It uses simple language and examples to help understanding. Since it is open source, everyone can access, use, and share it under the CC BY-NC-ND 4.0 license.

The book explains concepts step by step without assuming prior knowledge.
